Jawahar](https://www.iiit.ac.in/people/faculty/jawahar/) and [Dr. Yashaswi Verma](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yashaswi-verma-91b868a5/). Prior to this, I worked as a software developer on a variety of web and desktop based applications. 

I have a broader research interest in the fields of Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Neural Networks. My Master's thesis was based on 'Automatic Image Annotation', where I studied the problem of automatically predicting a set of textual labels that best describe the semantics of an image.
