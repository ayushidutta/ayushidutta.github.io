I am graduating as a Masters by research student at [Centre for Visual Information Technology (CVIT)](https://cvit.iiit.ac.in/), [IIIT Hyderabad](https://www.iiit.ac.in/) in 2018. My masters has been advised by [Prof C.V. Jawahar](https://www.iiit.ac.in/people/faculty/jawahar/) and [Dr. Yashaswi Verma](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yashaswi-verma-91b868a5/). Prior to this, I was a software developer, having worked on multiple business applications mostly web based. 

I have a broader research interest in the fields of Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Neural Networks. My Master's thesis is based on 'Automatic Image Annotation', where I studied the problem of automatically predicting a set of textual labels that best describe the semantics of an image.
